About Housemate

More than a real estate agency — we're your trusted partners in building a better future, one property at a time

Our Story

Housemate Realtors was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every property transaction should be transparent, stress-free, and rewarding. Founded in Pune, we started as a small consultancy with a passion for connecting people with their perfect properties.

Over the years, we've grown into a full-service real estate firm — offering everything from buying and selling, to rentals, interior design, and society redevelopment. What hasn't changed is our commitment to honesty, expertise, and putting our clients first.

Today, we're proud to be one of Pune's most trusted names in real estate, with a track record of 500+ successful transactions and a growing family of satisfied clients across the city.
