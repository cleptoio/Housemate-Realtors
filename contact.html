Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries about our services

What areas in Pune do you cover?

We cover all major areas including Baner, Hinjewadi, Kothrud, Wakad, Aundh, Balewadi, Viman Nagar, Hadapsar, and surrounding neighborhoods. Our network extends across Pune city and Pimpri-Chinchwad.

Are your properties RERA registered?

Yes, we exclusively deal with RERA-registered properties. We verify all legal documents and certifications before listing any property to ensure complete transparency and security for our clients.

Do you charge for property consultations?

Initial consultations are completely free. We discuss your requirements, budget, and preferences to understand how we can best serve you — with zero obligation.

How does the interior design service work?

We offer end-to-end interior design: consultation, 3D design visualization, material selection, execution, and handover. Projects typically take 45-90 days depending on scope. We handle modular kitchens, wardrobes, full home interiors, and commercial spaces.

Can you help with society redevelopment?

Absolutely. We provide complete redevelopment support — from feasibility study and FSI analysis to builder selection, legal documentation, member consensus building, and project monitoring until final possession.
